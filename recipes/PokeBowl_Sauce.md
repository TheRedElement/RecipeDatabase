# Poke Bowl (Sauce)
![ 300](../gfx/PXL_20250413_031221242.jpg)

|Ingredient|Amount (4 portions)|
| :- | :- |
|oil|240 mL|
|soy sauce|240 mL|
|vinegar|6 mL|
|lemon|4|
|garlic|-|
|ginger|-|

## Recipe
1. combine **soy-sauce**, **oil**, **vinegar (rice)**, **lemon**, **garlic**, **ginger** in small bowl
2. whisk until well mixed
