# Sauce al Ragu

## Ingredients

## Recipe
1. chop **onion** and **garlic**
1. grate **carrot**
1. heat **oil** in a pot/pan
1. roast **minced meat**, **onion**, **garlic**, **carrot**
1. add **tomatoe**, **tomato-sauce**, **tomato-paste**
1. season with **oregano**, **basil**, **thyme**, **salt**, **pepper**
1. let simmer for ~10min
