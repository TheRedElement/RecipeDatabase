# Spaghetti

|Ingredient|Amount (4 portions)|
| :- | :- |
|pasta|500 g|
|salt|-|
|water|-|

## Recipe
1. boil salted **water**
1. add **spaghetti**
1. reduce to medium-high heat
1. let boil until **spaghetti** are al dente
