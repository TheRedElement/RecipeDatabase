## Recipe
1. cook [pasta](Pasta.md)
2. finely chop **garlic**, **parsley**
3. roast **garlic** and **parsley** in **oil (olive)**
4. once [pasta](Pasta.md) is done, add to pan
    1. swirl [pasta](Pasta.md) around until everything is covered in sauce (oil)
