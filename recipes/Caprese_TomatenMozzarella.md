## Recipe
1. chop **tomatoes** into slices
1. chop **mozzarella** into slices
1. chop **basil**
1. put everything into one bowl
1. season with **(olive) oil**, **balsamico**, **salt**, **pepper**
1. serve with **bread (white)**
