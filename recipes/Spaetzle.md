# Spätzle

|Ingredient|Amount (4 portions)|
| :- | :- |
|flour|300 g|
|milk|150 mL|
|butter|50 g|
|egg|3|
|salt|0.4 g|
|water|-|

## Recipe
1. melt **butter**
1. mix with **milk**, **salt**, **eggs**
1. mix in **flour**
    1. add **milk** if dough is to viscous
1. boil salted **water** in pot
    1. add dough via
        1. spätzle-sieb
    1. take dough out of **water** when swimming to surface
