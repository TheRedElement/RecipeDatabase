# Fried Egg

## Recipe
1. add **oil** to pan
2. add **egg**
	1. make sure egg-yolk does not break
3. let fry for a few minutes
	1. until egg-white solidifies
	2. egg-yolk shall still be ooozy
4. optionally season with **salt**, **pepper**, **chili flakes** while frying
