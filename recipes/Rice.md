# Rice

|Ingredient|Amount (4 portions)|
| :- | :- |
|rice|800 g|
|water|1000 mL|
|salt|-|

## Recipe
1. add **rice** to pot
1. add **salt**
1. add **water** (double the amount of **rice**)
1. let boil until **rice** absorbes all the water
