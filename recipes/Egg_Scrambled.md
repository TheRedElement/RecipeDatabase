## Ingredients

## Recipe
1. chop **onions**, **garlic**
1. (if **bacon cubes**)
    1. roast in pan
1. add **oil** to pan
1. add **garlic**, **onions** into pan
    1. roast gently
1. add **egg** and **parmesan** into pan
1. mix everything together
1. season with **salt** and **pepper**

## Side
* dark bread


## Notes
* also other **vegetables** can be added
