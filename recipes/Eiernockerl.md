## Recipe

1. melt **butter**
2. mix with **milk**, **salt**, **eggs**
3. mix in **flour**
    1. add **milk** if dough is to viscous    
4. boil salted **water** in pot
    1. add dough via
        2. forming small bunches with 2 tablespoons
    2. take dough out of **water** when swimming to surface
