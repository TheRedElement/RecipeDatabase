# Papaya Seeds

## Ingredients

## Recipe
1. rinse **papaya** seeds
1. dry **papaya** seeds until dry by touch
    1. at low temperature in oven
    1. in a pan, tossing regularly
1. grind to powder
    1. using mortar
    1. using blender

## Notes
* results in powder that tastes
    * peppery
    * a little like **cress (water**)
