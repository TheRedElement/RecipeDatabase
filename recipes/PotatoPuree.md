# Potato Puree

## Ingredients

## Recipe
1. wash, peel, and cut **potato** into pieces
1. cook **potato** in salted **water** until soft
1. mash cooked **potato**
1. add **butter** and **milk**
1. heat up
1. season with **salt** and **nutmeg**
1. whisk until homogeneous
